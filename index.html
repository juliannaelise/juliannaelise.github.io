NFL Passing Analytics
R · nflfastR · 2019–2023 Seasons
Sports Analytics Regression & Correlation

Built an end-to-end study of quarterback performance using five years of play-by-play data, focusing on passing efficiency and decision-making. The project used feature engineering, exploratory visualization, and correlation analysis to surface patterns that could support talent evaluation and strategy.

  • Filtered and aggregated nflfastR data to isolate full-season QB stats and key KPIs such as attempts, completions, air yards, and yards after catch.
  • Quantified relationships like attempts vs. completions (with an almost perfect Pearson correlation) and air yards vs. YAC using Spearman correlation to handle outliers.
  • Translated technical results into plain-language insights for decision-makers, highlighting how passing volume and route depth affect offensive efficiency.
Airline Operations KPI Dashboard
Power BI · SQL · Excel
Operations Analytics Simulation

Designed an interactive dashboard to track delay and cancellation performance by route, carrier, and season. Combined SQL, Excel, and Power BI to help stakeholders see where delays originate and how schedule changes affect the system.

  • Wrote SQL queries to compute on-time rates, completion factors, and controllable delay minutes by route and airline.
  • Built a scheduling scenario that suggested roughly a double-digit percentage reduction in controllable delay minutes, illustrating the impact of better sequencing and buffers.
  • Presented metrics in a clean visual layout with drill-downs to support “what-if” conversations around network changes.
